For this series, I challenged myself to create at least three pieces of abstract art a day. I was only intending the project to last a few days, but I enjoyed the process so much I continued for over a year and more than a thousand images.

I had two rules:

  • No self-editing. I had to go with an idea without thinking too much about whether I liked it. This meant some pieces are better than others, but the goal was to practice creating quickly, not to create a perfect image.
  • Only use an iPhone. Restricting myself to a small screen and limited features meant that I had to focus on the idea, rather than getting caught up with the tool.
